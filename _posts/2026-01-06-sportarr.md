## Installation

**Default install:**
```bash
bash -c "$(wget -qLO - https://github.com/community-scripts/ProxmoxVE/raw/main/ct/sportarr.sh)"
```
**CPU:** 2 core(s) &nbsp;|&nbsp; **RAM:** 2048 MB &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Disk:** 8 GB &nbsp;|&nbsp; **OS:** Debian 13

## Configuration

Config file:
```
/opt/sportarr/.env, /opt/sportarr-data/config/config.xml
```

> **INFO:** The resources assigned to LXC are considered baseline. Please adjust to match your workload.
