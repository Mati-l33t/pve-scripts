## Installation

**Default install:**
```bash
bash -c "$(wget -qLO - https://github.com/community-scripts/ProxmoxVE/raw/main/ct/zabbix.sh)"
```

## Notes

<div class="info-callout">
  <i class="fas fa-info-circle"></i>
  <div>Database credentials: <code>cat ~/zabbix.creds</code></div>
</div>

<div class="info-callout">
  <i class="fas fa-info-circle"></i>
  <div>You can choose between Zabbix agent (classic) and agent2 (modern) during installation</div>
</div>

<div class="info-callout">
  <i class="fas fa-info-circle"></i>
  <div>For agent2 the PostgreSQL plugin is installed by default; all plugins are optional</div>
</div>

<div class="info-callout">
  <i class="fas fa-info-circle"></i>
  <div>If agent2 with NVIDIA plugin is installed in an environment without GPU, the installer disables it automatically</div>
</div>
