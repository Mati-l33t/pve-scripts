It will also allow you to monitor weekly pull-lists for items belonging to said watchlisted series to download, as well as being able to monitor and maintain story-arcs."

## Installation

**Default install:**
```bash
bash -c "$(wget -qLO - https://github.com/community-scripts/ProxmoxVE/raw/main/ct/mylar3.sh)"
```
**CPU:** 1 core(s) &nbsp;|&nbsp; **RAM:** 512 MB &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Disk:** 4 GB &nbsp;|&nbsp; **OS:** Debian 13

## Web Interface

Default port: `8090`
