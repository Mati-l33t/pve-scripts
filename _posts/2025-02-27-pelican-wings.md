## Installation

**Default install:**
```bash
bash -c "$(wget -qLO - https://github.com/community-scripts/ProxmoxVE/raw/main/ct/pelican-wings.sh)"
```
**CPU:** 2 core(s) &nbsp;|&nbsp; **RAM:** 4096 MB &nbsp;|&nbsp; **Disk:** 8 GB &nbsp;|&nbsp; **OS:** Debian 13

## Notes

> **INFO:** After installation, you need to use the Auto Deploy command generated by Pelican Panel and, after running the command, restart the Wings service with `systemctl restart wings.service`
